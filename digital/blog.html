⚡ Copy-Paste ChatGPT Prompts

Tap a prompt to copy it, then paste into ChatGPT, Gemini, or your favorite AI tool.

Content Idea Generator

Act as a content strategist. Give me 10 content ideas for a [niche] business targeting [audience]. For each idea, include a hook, a short outline, and a call-to-action.

Sales Page Writer

Write a high-converting sales page for [product name], a [short product description]. Target audience: [audience]. Include a headline, 3 benefit-driven sections, social proof placeholder, and a strong call-to-action.

Email Sequence Builder

Create a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers of [business name]. Each email should build trust, share value, and lead toward [product/offer]. Keep tone friendly and conversational.

Freelance Proposal

Write a personalized freelance proposal for a [job type] project. Client's need: [describe need]. Highlight relevant experience, propose a clear deliverable timeline, and end with a confident next step.

Social Media Captions

Generate 5 engaging social media captions for [platform] promoting [product/topic]. Include relevant hashtags and a hook in the first line for each caption.

Automation Workflow Planner

Act as an automation consultant. Suggest a step-by-step automation workflow using [tool, e.g. n8n or Zapier] to handle [task, e.g. lead follow-up]. Include triggers, actions, and tools needed.
